Help

- Open your old image with the Open button!

- Save Your masterpiece with the Save Button!

Ready to share the world? Press this Download Button!

Now comes with a preview!

- The pencil button lets you draw freely

- The Line button lets you draw straight lines

- Adjust your stroke sizes with this bar!

- The square button allows you to choose your color.

- Clicking the counter-clockwise arrow button undos your last change (shortcut: ctrl-z / cmd-z)

- Clicking the clockwise arrow button redos your undo (shortcut: ctrl-shift-z / cmd-shift-z)

- The Add Frame button lets you add a frame.

- Move between frames with the left and right arrows! (Shortcut: left/right arrow key)

- Wanna get rid of the current frame? use the Trash button!
